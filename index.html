About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Houston, advised by Prof. Feng Yan. I received my M.S. in Computer Science from Georgia Tech and my B.Eng. from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

My research focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs), Data Science Agents, and process-aware reasoning systems.

My recent work explores how to build reliable, modular, and evaluation-driven AI agents for real-world data science tasks, including benchmarking, tool use, and structured reasoning. I am particularly interested in agent architectures, knowledge integration, and scalable evaluation frameworks.

Feel free to reach out if you are interested in collaboration.
